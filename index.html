God never intended us to live alone. At Joseph fellowship, we strive for genuine relationship in Christ and compassion for each other and the community.

As a fellowship started out with 1.5 generation and second generation immigrants, we believe God is a barrier breaking God. The gospel is meant for everyone regardless of where you came from and the language you speak. We hope to meet you and begin our discipleship journey with Christ together.

To find out more, visit our Facebook Page or email/message one of our core members.
